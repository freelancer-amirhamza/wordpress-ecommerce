== Description ==

Ecommerce Bazaar is a modern, multipurpose, and fully responsive theme crafted for online stores, digital marketplaces, multi-vendor platforms, fashion boutiques, electronics shops, furniture stores, home decor outlets, grocery stores, gadget retailers, toy shops, jewelry stores, cosmetic shops, organic product stores, fitness gear outlets, and lifestyle brands. Designed with sleek layouts and intuitive navigation, it offers seamless integration with the WooCommerce plugin for managing product catalogs, secure checkout, payment gateways, shopping carts, and inventory control. Compatible with plugins like YITH WooCommerce Wishlist, WooCommerce Product Filter, and Mailchimp for email marketing, the theme enhances customer engagement and conversion rates. Its retina-ready, mobile-friendly design ensures smooth performance across all devices, while SEO optimization, fast loading speed, and clean code improve search visibility and user experience. Ecommerce Bazaar includes customizable product grids, featured product sliders, discount banners, call-to-action buttons, testimonial sections, and social media integration to promote brand awareness and drive sales. Ideal for entrepreneurs, startups, and established retailers, this theme provides a scalable and professional eCommerce solution for creating visually appealing, high-performing, and conversion-focused online shops that boost business growth and customer trust.

= Installation =
	
1. In your admin panel, go to Appearance -> Themes and click the 'Add New' button.
2. Type in ecommerce-bazaar in the search form and press the 'Enter' key on your keyboard.
3. Click on 'Install' button to install and 'Activate' button to use.
